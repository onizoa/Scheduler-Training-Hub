Autoloop Scheduler: Trainee Workflow Guide

Welcome! This guide outlines the core functions and mandatory procedures you will use daily in the **Autoloop Scheduler** to manage service appointments, customer data, and optimize shop efficiency.

Start Learning Workflow

Autoloop Core Training Modules

1. System Access

Login & Logout: This critical first step ensures system security and license availability. You will learn the proper procedure to **log in** with your credentials and how to securely **log out** when finished working.

2. Scheduling Operations

New Appointment: Your primary function. Learn the step-by-step process for booking service time, including confirming customer and vehicle data, detailing the service request, and assigning the appointment to an optimal **time slot** based on live shop capacity.

Rescheduling: Understand how to efficiently modify an existing booking. Correct **rescheduling** immediately updates the calendar, freeing up the old slot and securing the new one.

Canceling: Learn the required steps to properly **cancel** an appointment. This action ensures the reserved service time is instantly released back into the available inventory.

3. Customer & Vehicle Data

New Customer: For first-time clients, this module guides you through creating a complete and accurate **customer profile**, which is the required foundation for all service transactions.

Add Vehicle: Learn to maintain precise records by **adding new vehicles** to a customer's profile. Accurate vehicle association is key for correct service planning.

Vehicle History: You will learn how to access the integrated **Vehicle History**. This allows you to quickly review a vehicle's past service, recommended future work, and repair orders to provide knowledgeable customer advice during the booking process.

Honda Service Pass for 2024

The Honda Service Pass is a free maintenance plan every 2024 model will have. It includes all scheduled maintenance for the first two years or 24,000 miles, whichever comes first. Your oil changes, tire rotations, and routine maintenance are covered. And if you need any repairs during that time, they'll be free. The Service Pass is available on all new Honda models, including the Civic, Accord, CR-V, and Pilot. It's included in the price of the vehicle.

CDK Service Scheduler: Trainee Workflow Guide

Welcome! This guide outlines the core functions and mandatory procedures you will use daily in the CDK Service Scheduler to manage your service workflow, customer data, and shop efficiency.

Start Learning Workflow

CDK Core Training Modules

1. System Access and Foundation

Login & Logout: We start with the critical steps for accessing and securing the system. You will learn how to **log in** using your authorized credentials and the proper procedure for **logging out** to maintain data security and license availability.

2. Appointment Management

New Appointment: This is your primary function for booking service time. You will learn the process of locating customer records, verifying vehicle information, inputting service requests, and assigning the appointment to an optimal time slot based on shop availability.

Rescheduling: Understand the process for modifying an existing appointment. This function is vital for efficiently moving a service booking to a new date/time while immediately updating your capacity calendar to reflect the change.

Canceling: Learn how to correctly cancel a service appointment. Proper cancellation ensures the reserved service time is instantly released back into the system inventory, maximizing your shop's potential availability.

3. Data Integrity and Records

New Customer: For walk-ins or first-time callers not yet in the system, this module guides you through creating a complete and accurate customer profile, which forms the foundation of all future transactions.

Add/Delete Vehicle: You will learn how to maintain precise customer records by adding new vehicles (e.g., new purchases) or deleting outdated ones (e.g., trade-ins). Correct vehicle association is essential for service accuracy.

Vehicle History: Accessing the integrated **Vehicle History** is paramount for effective customer service. You will learn how to quickly review a vehicle's past repair orders, services performed, and recommended future work to provide knowledgeable advice during booking.

Chapter 2 ‚Äî Appointments & Customers

This chapter focuses on the daily tasks of working with customers. You will learn how to book new appointments, reschedule or cancel existing ones, and create new customer records when needed.

3 ‚Äî Booking a New Appointment

[Illustration: New Appointment ‚Äî click to enlarge]
  1. Click New Appointment. (Do not create a customer first.)
  2. Search for the customer by phone number (recommended), name, email, VIN, or confirmation code.
  3. Verify the customer and the vehicle (year/make/model). If there are multiple vehicles, pick the one with a VIN.
  4. Select the correct service or maintenance package.
  5. Choose the date, time, and service advisor as required by dealership policy.
  6. Review the appointment details and click Save/Confirm.
  7. Provide the customer with the appointment confirmation details (day, date, time, and advisor).

üìù Take Notes

Search first ‚Äî always try to find the customer before creating new records.

VIN is important for recalls and certain packages ‚Äî include it when possible.

4 ‚Äî Rescheduling an Appointment

[Illustration: Reschedule Appointment ‚Äî click to enlarge]
  1. Search for the customer's existing appointment.
  2. Click the green appointment slot to open the details panel.
  3. Edit the date, time or service details as needed.
  4. Confirm the rescheduled appointment and inform the customer.

üìù Take Notes

Ask the customer for any constraints (e.g., "No morning appointments") before confirming.

5 ‚Äî Cancelling an Appointment

[Illustration: Cancel Appointment ‚Äî click to enlarge]
  1. Search for the customer's appointment.
  2. Open the appointment from the calendar (green slot).
  3. Confirm with the customer that they want to cancel (offer to reschedule).
  4. If cancelling, proceed to cancel and provide confirmation details to the customer.

üìù Take Notes

When cancelling, always suggest alternate dates/times ‚Äî it reduces no-shows.

6 ‚Äî Creating a New Customer

[Illustration: Add New Customer ‚Äî click to enlarge]
  1. Search first. If no record is found, click Quick Add (or Add).
  2. Enter First Name, Last Name, and Phone Number (label Home/Mobile).
  3. Add vehicle information: Year/Make/Model, Mileage, and VIN if available.
  4. Save the customer and then continue to create the appointment.

üìù Take Notes

Use Quick Add for speed. Keep customer name spelling correct ‚Äî avoid changing names later.

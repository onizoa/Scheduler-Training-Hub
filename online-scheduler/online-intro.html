Online Scheduler: Trainee Workflow Guide

Welcome! This comprehensive guide provides the necessary training to effectively manage service bookings, customer data, and scheduling changes within the **Online Scheduler** platform.

Start Learning Workflow

Online Scheduler Core Training Modules

1. System Access

Access Website: The first step is navigating to the correct **Online Scheduler website** and successfully logging in to begin managing appointments.

2. Customer & Vehicle Records

New Customer: Learn the procedure for accurately creating a complete profile for any first-time client not yet in the system.

Add Vehicle: Understand how to associate a new vehicle correctly with an existing or new customer profile, ensuring service details are always accurate.

3. Scheduling Operations

New Appointment: Master the workflow for booking service time, including service selection, time slot assignment, and final confirmation.

Reschedule: Learn how to efficiently move an existing appointment to a new date or time, updating the calendar instantly.

Canceling: Understand the correct procedure for removing an appointment from the schedule, which immediately frees up the time slot for other customers.

CRITICAL STEP: Hot Alert Requirement

For both **Reschedule** and **Canceling** modules, sending a **Hot Alert** to the Dealer is a **mandatory and crucial step** to ensure immediate notification and proper documentation of the schedule change.
